Parameters
maxChargethe max charge the battery can hold in KWh.
initialChargePercentagethe initial charge percentage that the battery holds once the train is loaded to the network.
depthOfDischargethe allowable depth of discharge, the battery can drain to.
batteryCRatethe C-Rate of discharge, the recharge C-Rate is taken as 1/2 x C-Rate.
maxRechargeSOC(Optional) the maximum recharge State of Charge the battery reaches when recharging.
minRechargeSOC(Optional) the minimum allowed State of charge when the battery is being drained.
